The Brown Bear

About Brown Bear

The brown bear (Ursus arctus) is native to parts of northen Eurasia and Nort America. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.

There are many subspecies within the brown bear species, including the attlas bear and the himalayan brown bear.

Species

  • Collarus
  • Horribilis
  • Nelsoni (extinct)
